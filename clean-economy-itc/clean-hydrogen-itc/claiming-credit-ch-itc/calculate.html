Calculating the credit

Credit rates for capital cost of eligible Clean Hydrogen property

Your Clean Hydrogen ITC is calculated based on the specified percentage of the capital cost to you of eligible clean hydrogen property that becomes available for use in the year.

If you are a taxable Canadian corporation who is a member of a partnership (pop-up), your Clean Hydrogen ITC for a tax year also includes your reasonable share of the Clean Hydrogen ITC computed for the partnership for the taxation year.

Your credit rate depends on whether you elect to meet the labour requirements.

Refer to: Avoid the reduced tax credit rate

The credit rate is between 15% and 40% of the capital cost of eligible clean hydrogen property that you acquired for use in a qualified Clean Hydrogen project, depending on when the property is acquired and the carbon intensity (CI) of the hydrogen to be produced by the project. Processes with the lowest carbon intensity would earn the highest rate. The credit would not apply when the carbon intensity is 4.0 kg or more.

The credit rate is up to 15% in respect of the capital cost of certain clean ammonia equipment, or certain equipment described in subparagraphs (c)(iv) to (vi) of the definition “eligible clean hydrogen property” that is used solely in connection with clean ammonia equipment you acquired before 2034.

The rate is subject to several conditions, including the condition that the hydrogen to be produced by the project and used in the production of ammonia has an expected carbon intensity less than 4.

Clean Hydrogen ITC is unavailable if an eligible clean hydrogen property (or an interest in a person or partnership with a direct or indirect interest in such property) is a tax shelter investment under section 143.2 the Income Tax Act.

Regular credit rates for eligible clean Clean Hydrogen property (other than clean ammonia equipment or certain other equipment used solely in connection with clean ammonia equipment) that is acquired after March 27, 2023, and that becomes available for use on or before December 31, 2034

Expected Carbon intensity* need redesign this footnote Acquired and available for use after March 27, 2023 and before 2034 Acquired in 2034
Less than 0.75 40% 20%
0.75 or greater and less than 2 25% 12.5%
2 or greater and less than 4 15% 7.5%

The credit does not apply for years after 2034.

* For property acquired after the compliance period of a qualified Clean Hydrogen project, the expected carbon intensity is the greater of the expected carbon intensity otherwise determined and the actual carbon intensity for the compliance period.

The above chart does not apply to clean ammonia equipment. For clean ammonia equipment or certain other equipment used solely in connection with clean ammonia equipment acquired before 2034 the rate is 15%, if acquired in 2034 the rate is 7.5%, and 0% after 2034. These rates apply regardless of the carbon intensity, provided the carbon intensity is less than 4. The credit rate also depends on whether you elect to meet the labour requirements.
