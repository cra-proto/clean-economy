What is the Clean Electricity ITC

The Clean Electricity ITC is a refundable tax credit that applies to eligible clean electricity property that is acquired from March 28, 2023 and that becomes available for use on or before December 31, 2034.

This credit is related to the production of clean electricity with a carbon intensity of less than 4 kilograms of carbon dioxide equivalent (CO2e) per kilogram of electricity produced; and if applicable, the production of clean ammonia that uses a feedstock of clean electricity produced by the qualified project.

Clean electricity

Clean electricity means electricity produced, whether solely or in conjunction with other gases, that has a carbon intensity of less than four.

Carbon intensity

Carbon intensity means the quantity in kilograms of carbon dioxide equivalent per kilogram of electricity produced.
