Clean Electricity Investment Tax Credit (ITC)

The Clean Electricity ITC is a refundable tax credit to encourage the investment of capital in eligible clean electricity property in Canada.

The Clean Electricity ITC is administered by the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) and Natural Resources Canada (NRCan).

  • CRA provides:

    • Ability to claim the credit on a corporate income tax or trust return
    • Audit and compliance activities
    • Administration of the credit

  • NRCan provides:

    • o Engineering and scientific guidance on clean electricity property
    • For natural gas energy systems:
      • Natural gas energy system submission portal
      • System evaluations and verifications of property
      • Review of qualified natural gas energy systems and emission intensity reports
      • Review of compliance reports
      • Guidance on validation firm requirements
